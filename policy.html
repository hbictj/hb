Policy

Professional rules for a quality learning environment

These policies help maintain quality, discipline, transparency, and academic professionalism within the institute.

Admission Policy

Admissions are offered on merit, seat availability, and successful completion of the registration process. Students must provide accurate personal and academic information.

Attendance Policy

Regular attendance is strongly encouraged to ensure proper learning, practical performance, and timely completion of the course.

Fee Policy

Fee once submitted is managed according to institute rules. Students are advised to confirm fee schedule, due dates, and course details at the time of admission.

Code of Conduct

Students are expected to maintain discipline, respect faculty and classmates, and use institute equipment responsibly.

Certification Policy

Certificates are awarded after successful completion of required coursework, attendance expectations, and assessments where applicable.

Technology & Resource Policy

Lab resources, internet facilities, CCTV monitored areas, and academic materials are provided to support a safe and professional learning environment.
