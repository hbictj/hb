Basic Introduction to I.T

Simple English Note for Students

Information Technology, or I.T, means the use of computers and digital tools to create, store, process, and share information. In simple words, I.T helps people work with data and communication through machines and software.

A computer is an important part of I.T. It accepts input, processes data, stores information, and gives output. Input devices include the keyboard and mouse. Output devices include the monitor and printer.

There are two main parts of a computer system. The first is hardware, which means the physical parts that we can touch, such as the keyboard, monitor, CPU, and hard disk. The second is software, which means the programs and instructions that tell the computer what to do.

I.T is used in schools, offices, banks, hospitals, shops, and homes. It helps in typing documents, making calculations, sending emails, attending online classes, keeping records, and searching for information.

The internet is also a major part of I.T. It connects computers around the world and allows people to communicate, learn, and share information quickly.

In modern life, I.T is very important because it saves time, improves accuracy, and makes work easier. Students who learn I.T gain useful skills for education, office work, and future jobs.
