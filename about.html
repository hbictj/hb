About Institute

A professional institute for practical computer education

HB Institute of Communication & Technology Jacobabad is a professional educational institute dedicated to computer literacy, office technology, digital skills, and practical training. Since 2013, the institute has been supporting students with structured learning opportunities designed to improve academic, office, and career performance.

The institute serves male and female students and focuses on useful, job-oriented, and skill-based learning. Its environment is built around discipline, technology access, practical lab sessions, and academic support.

Students join HBICT to build confidence in digital tools, improve office skills, prepare for jobs, and strengthen their academic abilities through guided learning.
